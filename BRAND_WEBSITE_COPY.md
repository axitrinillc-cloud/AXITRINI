## DESIGN NOTES

### Typography
- **Headlines**: Bold, large (48-72px desktop), minimal font (Inter, SF Pro, or similar)
- **Body**: Clean, readable (16-18px), generous line-height (1.6-1.8)
- **Hierarchy**: Clear size differentiation, plenty of whitespace

### Color Palette
- **Primary**: Blue (#0ea5e9) - matches brand
- **Secondary**: Green (#22c55e) - matches brand
- **Neutral**: White, light gray (#f5f5f5), dark gray (#171717)
- **Accent**: Minimal use of brand colors for CTAs

### Spacing
- Generous padding (80-120px sections)
- Large margins between elements
- Breathing room around text

### Animations
- Smooth scroll-triggered fade-ins
- Subtle hover effects on CTAs
- Parallax on hero section (optional, subtle)

### Mobile Considerations
- Stack sections vertically
- Large touch targets (44px minimum)
- Readable font sizes (no smaller than 16px)
- Simplified navigation
